Some of the Animals here at Riget Zoo Adventures!

Lions
Population in the wild: 600 IUCN Status: Endangered Type: Mammals Region: Gujuart, India Habitat: Dry forest Facts: Lions are fearsome animals, as they are keystone predators in their habitat.

Baby Gorillas
Population in the wild: 316,000 IUCN Status: Critically Endangered Type: Mammals Region: Congo Habitat: Forest Facts: Baby gorillas are cute, soft and cuddly.

Penguins
Population in the wild: 28,300 IUCN Status: Vulnerable Type: Birds Region: Chile, Peru Habitat: Oceans and coastlines Facts: Penguins are expert divers and are cute when they waddle!

Giraffe
Population in the wild: Decreasing IUCN Status: Varied Type: Mammals Region: Southern Africa Habitat: Bushy Savannah Facts: Giraffes have a ginormous neck that makes them uniquely special but fear not, they are fast and dangerous!

Dolphin
Population in the wild: 600,000 IUCN Status: Critical Type: Mammals Region: Worldwide Habitat: Oceans and Seas Facts: Dolphins are very intelligent and can jump out of the water so majestically!

Sloth
Population in the wild: Unknown IUCN Status: Least concern Type: Mammals Region: Central and South America Habitat: Tropical forests Facts: Sloths are surprisingly good at swimming and are excellent tree hanging creatures!
