frequently asked questions

how to cancel?

Want to cancel? We hope not but if you do, either send us a message on the contact form or go to the reservation section and cancel from there. We hope you come again soon!

is there any vacancy?

You can check availability by filling out the reservation form and then clicking on the Check Availability button.

what are payment methods?

No payment needs to be made to the hotel as it is already included in your zoo visit booking.

how to claim the on-site benefits?

Everything is included within the booking! Feel free to use any of our services-families are welcome!

what are the age requirements?

No under 18s please in the bar area! Will need someone over 18 to check in at the reception desk! Remember to have your ID ready if needed! Other than that, children are welcome to explore anywhere they like! This hotel is family friendly-perfect for a family holiday!
